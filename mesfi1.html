About Me

He received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Hawassa University in 2012, Ethiopia, and his M.Sc. degree in Information Technology from Madras University in 2016, India. He received his Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). Currently, he is a postdoctoral fellow at the College of Mechatronics and Control Engineering, Shenzhen University.

Services

He served as a lecturer and a member of the postgraduate committee at Wachemo University, Ethiopia, from 2018-2022. He is also worked as a member of the Ubiquitous and Wireless Networks research team, at UESTC. His research interests include:- Unmanned aerial vehicle networks,5G/6G wireless networks, machine learning; vehicular ad hoc networks; intelligent transportation systems; cloud computing; mobile edge computing; multi-robot systems; vehicular networks, the Internet of Things, and mobile computing.
